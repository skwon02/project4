• Our CLass Offerings

  • Ballet

    Six Levels of Ballet begin for dancers ages 6-9 years, addressing basic traveling patterns and fundamental positions and movements.

  • Pointe

    Pre-Pointe is reserved for dancers who have reached Level 3 Ballet who seek the conditioning necessary for future Pointe work.

  • Modern

    Six Levels of Modern begin for new modern dancers ages 6-9 with basic technique.

  • Tap

    Tap is available for new tappers ages 4-5 up through the most advanced tappers in Level 3.

  • Jazz

    For students in Level 2 and beyond interested in learning about a variety of age-appropriate hip hop rhythms and styles.

  • Hip-Hop

    Two Levels of Jazz are available to dancers in Level 2 and beyond, seeking fun, fast-paced movement, jumps, leaps, and turns.

  • African

    Open to dancers ages 8 and older, this class is a blend of traditional African dance and Dunham Technique.

  • Little Ones

    For young students ages 3-5. This class will develop skill in rhythm, balance, elemental patterning, etc.

  • Repertory

    Designed for students, ages 8 and over in Level 2 and above, with a desire to dance and learn, at an accelerated pace,repertory material, which is reserved and often repeated as it would be the case in a professional company.

  • Adult Classes

    Class cards may be used for any of the adult classes on our schedule, and you are welcome to take multiple classes in one week!
